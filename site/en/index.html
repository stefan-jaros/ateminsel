Why breath therapy?

Your breath connects body, mind and soul. But stress, anxiety or illness throw it off balance. Breath therapy gently and sustainably releases these blockages.

Breathing difficulties

For hyperventilation, shortness of breath or shallow breathing, rediscover your natural breathing rhythm.

Panic attacks

Calm anxiety and panic through conscious breathing and stabilize the nervous system.

Stress and burnout

Break free from chronic stress, find calm and recharge your energy.

Chronic conditions

Improve quality of life alongside asthma, COPD and other conditions.

Rehabilitation

Bring the body back into balance after surgery, accident or illness.

Tension

Release muscular blockages and relieve pain through conscious breath movement.

High sensitivity

Discover your own sensitivity as a strength and manage it better in everyday life.

Mindfulness

Strengthen body awareness, perceive the moment and find inner peace.
